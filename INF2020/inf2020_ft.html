Final Test

 

This supervised test will assess your keyboarding speed, accuracy, and technique. Before the timed portion of your test begins, spend a few moments preparing yourself. Adjust your chair, keyboard, and monitor for optimum comfort and efficiency. After each timed test, you will be asked to print a copy of your documents. Ensure that you are in a place that allows you to do so.

 

Remember that you will also be evaluated on your technique by your teacher. See your teacher for access to this test.

Toolkit

 

 

The toolkit is a support for you in CTS. In it will be resources to help clarify ideas and support you as you work through your course. Anytime there is a toolkit item for you to access, you will see this icon.

 

All of the support resources in this toolkit will be available at any time as you work through this course. By housing these resources here, you can easily find specific support resources without having to traverse through the course.

 

In this toolkit are the following items:

  • Workstation Routines Rubric

  • Basic Competencies Rubric

  • Project 1 Assessment

  • Project 2 Assessment

  • Technique Evaluation

  • Instructional Videos
    – Creating a Folder on Your Desktop
    – Setting Tabs in Microsoft Word®
    – Creating and Saving a File
    – The Header and Footer of a Document

  • Alphanumeric Review

  • Numeric Review

  • Looking Back

Project 3: Documents for Personal and Business Use

 

Comments

 

The Comments feature allows you to makes notes within a document. Comments enable writers and reviewers to call attention to an item in a document, make a point, or even ask a question, without interfering with the document’s content. Comments are automatically labeled with the user’s initials and a comment number. You will find the Comments group beside the Tracking group in the Review tab on the ribbon.

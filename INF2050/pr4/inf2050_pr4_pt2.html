Final Project 2: Fax Template

 

When you fax documents, you always need to include a fax cover page to provide the recipient with information about the documents you have sent. In this part of the final project, you will create a fax cover page that you can use to send your resumé package to prospective employers.

 

Using the built-in templates in Microsoft Word, create a professional-looking fax cover page:

 

Step 1: In Microsoft Word’s Fax template, enter all relevant information to send your fax to Smartech Strategic Technical Support.

Step 2: Use Insert Symbol to check off how you would like the receiver to respond (e.g., Urgent, For Review, Please Comment, Please Reply, etc.).

Step 3: Insert a brief comment expressing your interest in the position and requesting an interview.

Step 4: Save the template as “Fax” in your INF2050: WP 2: Final Project folder.

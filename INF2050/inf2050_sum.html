Course Summary

 

Congratulations! You have completed INF2050: Word Processing 2, the second of three courses towards becoming certified in Microsoft Word.

 

INF3060: Word Processing 3 teaches advanced-level skills to prepare you to write the Microsoft Word Certification exam.

 

Consider taking more courses in Information Processing to learn the other Microsoft Office programs, as well as how to integrate the software suite.

 

You may also consider other strands in the Business, Administration, and Information Technology Pathway, such as:

  • Networking
  • Enterprise and Innovation
  • Management and Marketing
  • Financial Management
  • Computing Science

Discuss course options with your teacher to develop a Pathway Plan for your future.

Course Summary

 

Congratulations on completing INF1030: Word Processing 1. In completing this course you have developed introductory-level word-processing skills that will lead you towards Microsoft Certification.

 

Consider taking more modules in information processing to learn the other Microsoft Office programs as well as how to integrate the software suite.

 

You may also consider other strands in the Business, Administration, and Information Technology pathway, such as:

  • Networking
  • Enterprise and Innovation
  • Management and Marketing
  • Financial Management
  • Computing Science

Discuss module options with your teacher to develop your Pathway Plan for your future.

Project 3—Documents for Personal Use

 

Project Summary

 

When you can use word-processing software to produce professional-looking documents quickly and easily, you have developed skills that you will use in all aspects of your life. Consider the benefits of saving time while creating quality work. Think of how you will use your skills in your personal life, in your other school subjects, and as you enter post-secondary education or the workforce. People with skills stand out. Be one of those people.

 

This course is designed to honour the time it takes you to learn new skills. When you are comfortable with the skills presented in the training rooms, move on to your final project.

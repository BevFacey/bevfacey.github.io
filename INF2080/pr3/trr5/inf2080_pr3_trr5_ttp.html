Training Room 5: Collaborating and Securing Data

 

Time to Practise 5

 

Instructions

 

In this exercise you will apply all of the knowledge and skills covered in Training Room 5.

 

In Training Room 5 you learned how to save and share your Excel spreadsheet in different formats. Time to Practise 5 will provide you with different situation in which you will need to determine which of the methods you learned would be most appropriate. You will also need to provide your reasoning behind each decision you make.

 

You may want to refer to the training room for support or have a discussion with a friend, a classmate, or your teacher. Complete Time to Practise 5 Questions now. Save the completed document to your INF2080 SS2 folder.

 

Once you’ve completed the Time to Practice 5 questions, open your Software Skills Checklist and complete the chart for the skills you developed in this training room. You should have all the skills checked now.

 

 

Once you are finished and have saved your work, submit your Time to Practise 5 to your teacher for assessment.

 

If needed, revisit Training Room 5 to review any of the skills in which you had difficulties. If you are confident in your skills and abilities, then move on to Project 4—your final project.

Project 3: Spreadsheet Skills

 

Project 3 Summary

 

Now that you have completed the five training rooms and Time to Practise exercises, you have become more advanced in your spreadsheet skills. You created more complicated formulas to calculate payments and interest, looked up information within workbooks, created more detailed charts, and so on. You have added valuable skills to your skill set that will be with you for years to come. Think of how you will use your spreadsheet skills in your personal life, in other school subjects, and as you enter post-secondary or the workforce. People with skills stand out; are you one of those people?

 

You should be confident with your newly acquired skills and ready move on to your final project. Good luck!

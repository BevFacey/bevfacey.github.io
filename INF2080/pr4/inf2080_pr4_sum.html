Project 4: Final Project

 

Project 4 Summary

 

Project 4 required you to put together all of the skills you have learned throughout the course and apply them to five different assignments. 

 

In Part 1 you analyzed marks for French 20 and developed a professional-looking spreadsheet. In Part 2 you went car shopping and crunched the numbers to make an informed decision as to what rates, interest, and payments would work best for your budget. In Part 3 you looked at stock quotes and had to make recommendations to buy or sell stock. In Part 4 you applied the LOOKUP and vLOOKUP functions for your supervisor to compare sales numbers in your insurance firm. Finally, you completed the Career Connections assignment to learn more about your goals, values, and skills.

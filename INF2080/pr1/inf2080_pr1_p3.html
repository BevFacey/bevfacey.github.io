Practising good workstation positions, routines, and security quickly becomes a habit when you practise these skills and techniques every time you sit at a computer. Using good routines and practices will make you more productive and reduce the likelihood of injury; protecting your equipment and personal work from loss or damage will also save you time, money, and frustration.

 

Remember, continue to self-evaluate and reflect on your workstation management and correct routines to obtain optimum health, safety, and security. You will be evaluated on your workstation management by your teacher at the end of the course.

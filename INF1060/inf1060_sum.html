Course Summary

 

Congratulations on finishing INF1060:Spreadsheet 1! In working through this course, you have covered many spreadsheet skills that you will be able to use in your personal life, school studies, and possibly even your job. You now know how to manipulate data, perform calculations using formulas, and create colorful graphs to present information. Besides the spreadsheet skills themselves, you continue to develop your workstation routines and practices to promote safety and security as well as develop your employability soft skills. Making career connections is vital to maintaining a career focus. As you move through life, it is a good idea to keep completing interest and value inventories as your interest and skills change.

 

If this course held a high interest for you, you may want to consider completing INF2080: Spreadsheet 2.

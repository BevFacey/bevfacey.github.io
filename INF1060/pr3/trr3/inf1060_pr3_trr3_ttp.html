Training Room 3: Managing Spreadsheets

 

Time to Practise 3

 

Instructions

 

In this exercise you will apply all of the knowledge and skills covered in Training Room 3. Read through the following instructions to see what you need to do to complete Time to Practise 3.

 

Complete the following steps in order. Answer the questions posed in the Time to Practise 3 exercise as you complete the step.

 

Step 1: Open Time to Practise 3.

 

Step 2: Clear the contents of Rows 13, 14, and 15.

 

Step 3: Fix the spelling error in D5.

 

Step 4: Delete rows 17, 18, and 19.

 

Step 5: Delete columns F and G.

 

Step 6: Insert four rows above row 10.

 

Step 7: Insert two columns before column D.

 

Step 8: Increase the height of row 3 to 20 pts.

 

Step 9: Increase the width of all columns in which the text is not completely visible.

 

Step 10: Move column F into column E.

 

Step 11: Copy Cells A1:P24, and paste into a new worksheet tab. Call it COPY.

 

Step 12: In the Time to Practise 3 worksheet, select cells B6:B8 and cells A9:C9 and fill in the cells with a background colour.

 

Step 13: Select columns E and G and bold the text.

 

Step 14: Select rows 7, 14, and 29 and italicize the text.

 

Step 15: Convert text to columns for the NAME column. You will need to insert a column after the NAME column.

 

Step 16: Have the headings in the two columns created in Step 15 read LAST NAME and FIRST NAME.

 

Step 17: Save the document as Time to Practise 3 in your INF1060 SS1 folder.

 

Step 18: Open your Software Skills Checklist and complete the chart for the skills you developed in this training room.

 

 

Once you are finished and have saved your work, submit your Time to Practise 3 to your teacher for assessment.

 

If needed, revisit Training Room 3 to review any of the skills in which you had difficulties. If you are confident in your skills and abilities, then move on to Training Room 4.

Spreadsheets are powerful tools if you have the skills to use them. You are beginning to see that there is always more than one way to perform a command. Managing spreadsheets is about making the worksheet fit your needs. You may need to edit information, make the column wider, insert a row or a column, or move or copy cells quickly and efficiently.

 

In this training room you will

  • edit data
  • clear data
  • delete rows or columns
  • insert rows or columns
  • insert or delete cells
  • change column widths
  • change row heights
  • cut, copy, or move cells
  • convert text to columns
How to Do It

 

To successfully complete this training room, do the following:

  • Read and watch instructional videos of topics covered in the training room.
  • Use help and online references should you require additional instruction or software assistance.
  • Complete the Time to Practise 3 exercise.

 

You are now ready, once again, to start working in Microsoft® Excel® 2007. Open Training Room 3: Managing Spreadsheets, and follow the instructions, starting with editing cell information. Save your Training Room 3 in your INF1060 SS1 folder.

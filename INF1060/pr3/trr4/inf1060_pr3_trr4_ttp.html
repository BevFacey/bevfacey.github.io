Training Room 4: Formatting Spreadsheets

 

Time to Practise 4

 

Instructions

 

In this exercise you will apply all of the knowledge and skills covered in Training Room 4. Read through the following instructions to see what you need to do to complete Time to Practise 4.

 

Complete the following steps in order.

 

Step 1: Open Time to Practise 4.

 

Step 2: Center the four column headings—Name, Sales, Rate, and Commission—horizontally.  Make the headings 12-point, bolded text.

 

Step 3: Bold the text in cell A12, and make it 12-point text as well.

 

Step 4: Format the numeric values in columns C and E to currency ($) with a comma style number. (Refer to Training Room 2 if you need to review formatting numbers.)

 

Step 5: Format the numeric values in column D to percent with two decimal places.

 

Step 6: Fill cells A5:E5 and cells A12:E12 with a light shade of gray.

 

Step 7: Border cells A5:E5 and cells A12:E12 with a top and bottom border.

 

Step 8: Turn off the gridlines and headings.

 

Step 9: Change the color of the worksheet tab at the bottom.

 

Step 10: Select the table, and choose a table style and theme.

 

Step 11: Save the document as Time to Practise 4 in your INF1060 SS1 folder.

 

Step 12: Open your Software Skills Checklist and complete the chart for the skills you developed in this training room.

 

 

Once you are finished and have saved your work, submit your Time to Practise 4 to your teacher for assessment.

 

If needed, revisit Training Room 4 to review any of the skills in which you had difficulties. If you are confident in your skills and abilities, then move on to Training Room 5.

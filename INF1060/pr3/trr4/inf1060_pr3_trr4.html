Training Room 4: Formatting Spreadsheets

 

© Monkey Business Images/shutterstock

Data contained in spreadsheets is important. More importantly, though, is the organization of the data and your ability to format the data so it is esthetically pleasing to others. Spreadsheets are often used in presentations; so, being able to place borders around cells, colour-coding information, or even placing a company logo will make your work stand out.

 

In this training room you will

  • format data
  • align data
  • add borders
  • adjust colors and patterns
  • change worksheet tab colors
  • add a worksheet background
  • display or hide gridlines and headings
  • apply table themes
  • apply table styles
How to Do It

 

To successfully complete this training room, do the following:

  • Read and watch instructional videos of topics covered in the training room.
  • Use help and online references should you require additional instruction or software assistance.
  • Complete the Time to Practise 4 exercise.

 

You are now ready, once again, to start working in Microsoft® Excel® 2007. Open Training Room 4: Formatting Spreadsheets, and follow the instructions, starting with formatting data. Save your Training Room 4 in your INF1060 SS1 folder.

Training Room 2: The Basics of Spreadsheets

Each training room you complete adds to the skill set you are developing. In Training Room 2 you will manipulate the look of a worksheet and discover how to select ranges of cells as well as navigate around the worksheet. You will begin entering information in the form of text, dates, and times and learn how to format information according to type and look.

 

In this training room you will

  • change the views of the worksheet
  • hide and unhide worksheets
  • zoom in and out of a worksheet
  • navigate through a worksheet
  • enter text, dates, and times
  • find and replace data
  • select cells
  • determine and enter cell ranges
How to Do It

 

To successfully complete this training room, do the following:

  • Read and watch instructional videos of the topics covered in the training room.
  • Use help and online references whenever you require additional instruction or software assistance.
  • Complete the Time to Practise 2 exercise.

 

You are now ready, once again, to start working in Microsoft® Excel® 2007. Open Training Room 2: The Basics of Spreadsheets, and follow the instructions, starting with changing views of a workbook. Save your Training Room 2 in your INF1060 SS1 folder.

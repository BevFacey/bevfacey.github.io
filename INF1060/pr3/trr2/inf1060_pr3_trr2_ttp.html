Training Room 2: The Basics of Spreadsheets

 

Time to Practise 2

 

Instructions

 

In this exercise you will apply all of the knowledge and skills covered in Training Room 2.

 

Read through the following instructions to see what you need to do to complete Time to Practise 2.You can either print these instructions or you can have them open alongside the Time to Practise 2 spreadsheet on your computer screen and work through them simultaneously.

 

Complete the following steps in order. Answer the questions posed in the Time to Practise 2 exercise as you complete the step.

 

Step 1: Open Time to Practise 2.

 

Step 2: Change the Zoom control to 78% and then back to 100%.

 

Step 3: Use the scroll bars to move up and down one row at a time.

 

Step 4: Using the most efficient method, go to row 143. Which method did you use?

 

Step 5: Using a key combination, move to cell A1. Which key combination did you use?

 

Step 6: Using a key combination, move to the last cell. Which key combination did you use?

 

Step 7: Using the Name Box, go to cell C132. What is in cell C132?

 

Step 8: Select cells B4:B6 and A8:C9.

 

Step 9: Select columns B and D separately.

 

Step 10: Select rows 7, 10, and 14 altogether.

 

Step 11: Reformat the cells that contain dates, to a format like January 1, 2010.

 

Step 12: Find all occurrences of 2010 and replace it with a 2009.

 

Step 13: In cell A1, insert the current time and format it so AM or PM appears.

 

Step 14: Change the view of the worksheet. What is the date in the last row of page 1?

 

Step 15: Save the document as Time to Practise 2 in your INF1060 SS1 folder.

 

Step 16: Open the Software Skills Checklist and complete the chart for the skills you developed in this training room.

 

 

Once you are finished and have saved your work, submit your Time to Practise 2 to your teacher for assessment.

 

If needed, revisit Training Room 2 to review any of the skills in which you had difficulties. If you are confident in your skills and abilities, then move on to Training Room 3.

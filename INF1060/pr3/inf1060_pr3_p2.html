Project 3: Spreadsheet Skills

 

Time to Work

 

 

Most of the work involved in this course occurs in this project, which includes working through eight training rooms and completing eight Time to Practise exercises.

 

Each of the eight training rooms should take approximately 2 hours to complete. Depending on your skill and experience, you may work through some training rooms faster than others. Efficiently working through the training rooms will ensure that you have enough time to complete this entire course.

                                             

Checking Your Work

 

Once you have completed the training rooms, complete the Time to Practise exercises that accompany them. The Time to Practise exercises are provided for you to practise the skills covered in the training room. Make sure you contact your teacher if you have questions about this process or need help completing any of the work.

 

Use the INF1060: Spreadsheet 1 Software Skills Checklist to evaluate your level of understanding. Check off the skills you are able to demonstrate as you progress through the training rooms. You must be competent in all software skills presented in the training rooms before beginning the final projects. Print or save a copy of the software checklist now. The checklist can also be found in the Toolkit.

 

You can repeat training rooms and demonstrations as necessary if you encounter difficulties with the software or with the Time to Practise exercises. You can also refer to Microsoft® Help and Online references for software training and demonstrations. If you experience any road blocks, contact your teacher for help and support.

 

Extending Beyond

 

INF1060: Spreadsheet 1 is one of many courses related to the business world. You may be interested in completing INF2080: Spreadsheet 2 next, as these two courses will set you well on your way to possible Microsoft Certification.

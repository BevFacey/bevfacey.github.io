Training Room 8: Charts

 

© Sean Gladwell/shutterstock

The ability to compare data and evaluate changes in data is easily done using charts. Charts are visual representations of data. They can quickly show the reader, for example, cost comparisons of various items, the performance of each salesperson at a dealership, or the amount of inventory in each department of an organization. In presentations, charts are very powerful tools when reporting data to a group of people or to an organization. It is important to know how to develop charts to accurately represent the data you wish to showcase.

 

In this training room you will

  • study charts and chart types
  • discover the effects of changing the data in a chart
  • move charts
  • resize charts
  • create a pie chart
  • change chart layouts
  • format charts
  • add and modify graphics
  • print charts

Training Room 8: Charts

 

Time to Practise 8

 

Instructions

 

In this exercise you will apply all of the knowledge and skills covered in Training Room 8. Read through the following instructions to see what you need to do to complete Time to Practise 8.

 

Complete the following steps in order. You will create charts on two separate worksheets: the Pie Chart worksheet and the Columnar Chart worksheet.

 

Step 1: Open Time to Practise 8.

 

On the Pie Chart worksheet, complete these steps:

 

Step 2: Create a pie chart of the expenses for What a Wonderful World Travel. Do not include the Totals. Choose the type of pie chart you like.

 

Step 3: Title the pie chart “Expenses.”

 

Step 4: Portray the data labels as percents. Go into Quick Layout to change their format in the Design tab.

 

Step 5: Resize the chart appropriately, and place it below the table.

 

Step 6: Explode the pie piece that depicts Advertising expense.

 

Step 7: Format the background appropriately.

 

On the Columnar Chart worksheet, complete these steps:

 

Step 8: Create a columnar chart for What a Wonderful World Travel that highlights Brockhoff’s information.

 

Step 9: Title the columnar chart “Brockhoff’s Expenses.”

 

Step 10: Remove the legend.

 

Step 11: Format the labels for Auto Rental, Air Fare, etc., to 10-point Arial bold.

 

Step 12: If needed, resize the chart so the labels are easy to read.

 

Step 13: Format the bars to make them red with a shadow. Use the Quick Layout in the Design tab to do this.

 

Step 14: Turn off the gridlines.

 

Step 15: Format the background of the whole chart so that the axes are easy to read.

 

Step 16: Save the document as Time to Practise 8 in your INF1060 SS1 folder.

 

Step 17: Open your Software Skills Checklist and complete the chart for the skills you developed in this training room.

 

 

Once you are finished and have saved your work, submit your Time to Practise 8 to your teacher for assessment.

 

Congratulations! You have now completed Project 3: Spreadsheet Skills. If needed, revisit Training Room 8 to review any of the skills in which you had difficulties. If you are confident in your skills and abilities, then move on to Project 4.

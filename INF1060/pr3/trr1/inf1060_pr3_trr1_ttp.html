Training Room 1: Introduction to Spreadsheets

 

Time to Practise 1

 

Instructions

 

In this exercise you will apply all of the knowledge and skills covered in Training Room 1.

 

Read through the following instructions to see what you need to do to complete Time to Practise 1.You can either print these instructions or you can have them open alongside the Time to Practise 1 spreadsheet on your computer screen and work through them simultaneously.

 

Complete the following steps in order:

 

Step 1: Open Time to Practise 1.

 

Step 2: Rename the Slave Lake worksheet Edmonton.

 

Step 3: Delete the Three Hills worksheet.

 

Step 4: Insert a worksheet before Calgary.

 

Step 5: Rename the worksheet you inserted in Step 4 with the name of your hometown.

 

Step 6: Put all the worksheets in alphabetical order.

 

Step 7: Make a copy of the Edmonton worksheet, and place it after the original Edmonton worksheet.

 

Step 8: Adjust the tab window size so that you can see as many of the tabs as possible.

 

Step 9: In cell A1 of each worksheet, create a hyperlink to the next worksheet using the word NEXT. Have the last worksheet link back to the first worksheet.

 

Step 10: Save the document as Time to Practise 1 in your INF1060 SS1 folder.

 

Step 11: Open the Software Skills Checklist and complete the chart for the skills you developed in this training room.

 

 

Once you are finished and have saved your work, submit your Time to Practise 1 to your teacher for assessment.

 

If needed, revisit Training Room 1 to review any of the skills in which you had difficulties. If you are confident in your skills and abilities, then move on to Training Room 2.

Training Room 5: Layout and Printing

 

Time to Practise 5

 

Instructions

 

In this exercise you will apply all of the knowledge and skills covered in Training Room 5. Read through the following instructions to see what you need to do to complete Time to Practise 5.

 

Complete the following steps in order. In steps 5 and 6, add your answer to the questions posed to the worksheet.

 

Step 1: Open your solution file for Time to Practise 4 in your INF1060 SS1 folder. Save this file as Time to Practise 5 in your INF1060 SS1 folder.

 

Step 2: Create a header with the title Time to Practise 5. Center and bold this header.

 

Step 3: Create a footer with your name centered.

 

Step 4: Change the page orientation to landscape.

 

Step 5: Check the page to ensure that all the information fits on one page. Which method did you use to preview your page?

 

Step 6: Print only the table in your worksheet. To print only the table, what do you need to do in the Print dialogue box?

 

Step 7: Save the document as Time to Practise 5 in your INF1060 SS1 folder.

 

Step 8: Open your Software Skills Checklist and complete the chart for the skills you developed in this training room.

 

 

Once you are finished and have saved your work, submit your Time to Practise 5 to your teacher for assessment.

 

If needed, revisit Training Room 5 to review any of the skills in which you had difficulties. If you are confident in your skills and abilities, then move on to Training Room 6.

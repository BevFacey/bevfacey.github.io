But wait! The surprised look on your face turns serious when you learn there’s a catch! You are informed that the total cost of all the items you select must not exceed (go over) $1000.00, GST included. Also, the total cost of all the items (GST included) must fall between $995.00 and $1000.00 or you lose everything.

 

“Not a problem!” you exclaim. “I have a secret weapon called a spreadsheet! I can make it do the math for me, right down to the very last penny! Show me the money!”

 

Use the Internet to find the items you want. Shop places like Best Buy, Walmart, Costco, West 49, etc. When you are ready to start, open the Most Wanted spreadsheet and follow the instructions provided to format the spreadsheet correctly. You can keep your spreadsheet open on the screen and your browser loaded with the websites you are using. This will allow you to work more efficiently if you need to switch back and forth between the two programs to check new prices online or to delete items that cost too much from your spreadsheet.

 

Continue spending your $1,000.00 on your Most Wanted items. Don’t forget that the total cost of all the items (tax included) must fall between $995.00 and $1000.00 or you lose all the money.

 

Save your Most Wanted spreadsheet in your INF1060 SS1 folder.

Project 4: Final Project

 

Before you start the final project, make sure you are confident in demonstrating all the skills covered in the training rooms of Project 3. Start by reviewing your Software Skills Checklist to determine your readiness for the final project. Then complete the Time to Reflect portion of your Checklist, and submit it to your teacher.

 

Thus far, you have learned some important skills in spreadsheets, and now it’s time to demonstrate these skills.

 

Discuss with your teacher a method for submitting your final project. Will it be presented in print or will it be digitally presented?

 

What do I already know?

 

By completing the eight training rooms and their Time to Practise exercises, you have learned the necessary content and software skills to complete the final projects.

 

What do I need to do?

  • create a Final Project folder in your INF1060 SS1 folder
  • create the following subfolders: Most Wanted, Personal Expenses, and Career Connections
  • complete all three parts of the final project
  • re-evaluate your workstation routines by completing the Workstation Routines Rubric and submit it with your final project
  • re-assess your soft skills by completing the Basic Competencies Rubric and submit it with your final project
Time to Work

 

 

This project will take approximately 5 hours.

 

The Final Project consists of three parts: Most Wanted, Personal Expenses, and Career Connections. In each part you will be provided a scenario and instructions as to what you will need to do. Once all three parts are completed, you will submit your Final Project to your teacher for assessment.

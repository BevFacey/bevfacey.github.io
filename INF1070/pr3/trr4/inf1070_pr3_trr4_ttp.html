Training Room 4: Customization, Links, and Effects

 

Time to Practise 4

 

Instructions

 

Now customize and add life to your Career Connections presentation by adding links, animations and transitions, and media clips.

 

Step 1: Open your Career Connections slide show (named careers.pptx).

 

Step 2: Apply the following:

  • a footer on all pages with the date, your name, and slide number
  • a background
  • a hyperlink
  • an action button
  • sounds and at least one movie
  • animations and transitions to slides

Remember to save your changes as you work and when you are done.

 

 

Checkpoint

 

Your Career Connections slide show should now include formatted text, visual content, and customized slides with backgrounds, links, and media clips.

 

Once you are finished Time to Practise 4 and have saved your work, talk with your teacher regarding assessment.

 

Retrieve the Software Skills Checklist that you saved to your computer. Check off the skills you have learned in this training room and that you are confident in demonstrating. Don’t forget to resave your Checklist.

 

If needed, revisit Training Room 4 to review any of the skills in which you had difficulties. If you are comfortable demonstrating the skills presented in this training room, continue to Training Room 5.

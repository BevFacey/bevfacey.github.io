Researching and gathering the content for your slides is the first step to creating your presentation. Building and formatting your slides to convey information in a meaningful and appealing way is the next step. In Training Room 1 you completed a career investigation and you built your information into slides. Training Room 2 focuses on formatting and manipulating your slides to improve the way they look.

 

What will I learn in Training Room 2?

 

In Training Room 2 you will

  • copy, cut, and paste text
  • format and modify textboxes, fonts, styles, size, and colour
  • align text and change line spacing
  • promote and demote text
  • add and modify bulleted and numbered lists
  • add and modify WordArt
  • apply themes

The estimated completion time for Training Room 2 and Time to Practise 2 is two hours.

 

How can I improve my learning?

 

To really grasp what you are learning, you can

  • read and watch demonstrations in Microsoft PowerPoint 2007 to discover how to format and manipulate slide content

  • use the Office Online Help and How-to website, should you require additional instruction or software assistance

  • complete all practice activities to learn the software

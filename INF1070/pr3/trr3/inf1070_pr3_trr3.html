In Time to Practise 1 and 2, you researched content, entered text, and modified and formatted slide layouts and content. Now it is time to add graphic objects that will draw in your audience, strengthen your message, and make your presentation more memorable for your audience.

 

What will I learn in Training Room 3?

 

In Training Room 3 you will

  • insert graphics, shapes, and other objects
  • modify graphics, shapes, and other objects
  • arrange graphics and other content
  • insert and modify charts
  • insert and modify tables

The estimated completion time for Training Room 3 and Time to Practise 3 is three hours.

 

How can I improve my learning?

 

To really grasp what you’re learning, you can

  • read and watch demonstrations in Microsoft PowerPoint 2007® to discover how to work with visual content in a presentation
  • use the Office Online Help and How-to website should you require additional instruction or software assistance
  • complete all practice activities to learn the software

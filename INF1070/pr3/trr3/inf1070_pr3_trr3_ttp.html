Training Room 3: Working with Visual Content

 

Time to Practise 3

 

Instructions

 

In your Career Connections slide show (named careers.pptx), you have already entered and formatted slide content. Now it is time to add visual interest to your presentation by adding, modifying, and arranging illustrations, charts, and tables.

 

Step 1: Open your Career Connections slide show (named careers.pptx).

 

Step 2: Make the following enhancements:

  • Add Clip Art, pictures, or shapes.
  • Add SmartArt.
  • Add a chart or a table.
  • Apply styles and effects to graphics, charts, or tables.
  • Align and place objects in order.

Remember to save your changes as you work and when you are done.

 

 

Checkpoint

 

Your Career Connections slide show should now include the formatted text and visual content that is modified, suitably arranged, and includes the following:

  • Clip Art, pictures, or shapes
  • SmartArt graphics
  • a chart or a table
  • styles and effects

Once you are finished Time to Practise 3 and have saved your work, talk with your teacher regarding assessment.

 

Retrieve the Software Skills Checklist that you saved to your computer. Check off the skills you have learned in this training room and that you are confident in demonstrating. Don’t forget to resave your Checklist.

 

If needed, revisit Training Room 3 to review any of the skills in which you had difficulties. If you are comfortable demonstrating the skills presented in this training room, continue to Training Room 4.

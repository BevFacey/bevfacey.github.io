Training Room 5: Collaborating and Delivering a Presentation

 

Time to Practise 5

 

Instructions

 

Now review your Career Connections presentation by collaborating with a peer for a constructive critique. Make any final edits necessary, and then prepare to package your presentation. Discuss with your teacher how you will deliver your presentation.

 

Step 1: Use the Spelling command and review your presentation to ensure your message is clear and complete.

 

Step 2: Rehearse and save timings.

 

Step 3: Have a reviewer input comments using the Review tab to make helpful suggestions to improve your presentation. Incorporate the suggestions made by your reviewer in the final editing of the slide show.

 

Step 4: Use the Document Inspector to find and remove hidden information.

 

Step 5: Save a copy of the presentation to be compatible with earlier versions of PowerPoint; name the file “Career Compatible.” Run the Compatibility Checker and make any necessary adjustments.

 

Step 6: Print a copy of your Career Connections presentation as Handouts, 6 slides per page. Add your name, INF1070 Digital presentation, and the date in a header on the handouts.

 

Step 7: Use the Office button to publish your presentation. Package your presentation in Copy to Folder.

 

Step 8: Discuss with your teacher the preferred method of presentation delivery.

 

 

Checkpoint

 

Your Career Connections PowerPoint presentation should now be complete and ready to present. Ensure that you have done the following tasks:

  • made final edits
  • rehearsed timings
  • used the Document Inspector
  • prepared your presentation to be packaged

Retrieve the Software Skills Checklist that you saved to your computer. Check off the skills you learned in this training room and that you are confident in demonstrating. If needed, revisit Training Room 5 to review any of the skills in which you had difficulties. Review any previous training rooms you feel necessary. Don’t forget to resave your Checklist.

 

Now that you are finished all five of the training rooms, ensure that your work is saved, and then print a copy of the Project Assessment Rubric, available in the Toolkit. Complete a self-assessment of your Career Connections presentation. Make any final changes necessary to your presentation.

 

Check in with your teacher to let your teacher know you are done this portion of the course, and to ask what you should do with your completed project and self-evaluation. The Project Assessment Rubric is the same evaluation tool that will be used to assess your final project. So, getting feedback from your teacher on your Career Connections project will help you in completing your final project.

 

When you feel comfortable demonstrating the skills in this course, begin Project 4: Final Project.

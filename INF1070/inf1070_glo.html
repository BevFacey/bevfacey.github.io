Course Glossary

 

carpal tunnel syndrome: a medical condition in which the median nerve is compressed at the wrist causing pain, weakness, or numbness in the hand or wrist that can radiate up the arm

 

repetitive strain injury: overuse of the muscles through repeated movements that puts stress on certain body parts, such as wrists or elbows

 

soft skill: a person’s personality, social refinement, language skills, or personal and/or work habits that contributes to efficient and successful productivity
